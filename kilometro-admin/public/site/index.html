Who We Are

About Kilometro Zero

Kilometro Zero Organization Incorporated is a non-stock organization focused on livelihood, inclusion, and community development.

20,000+

Members and beneficiaries supported through the organization’s growing network.

Creating Opportunities for Communities

Kilometro Zero serves as a starting point for hope, support, and opportunity. It helps members and beneficiaries gain access to livelihood programs, skills development, and community assistance.

The organization also supports Persons With Disabilities and works with government agencies, NGOs, and private partners to create sustainable impact.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision, and Goals

The organization’s work is guided by service, inclusion, and sustainable community development.

Mission

To engage in activities that improve the lives of members and beneficiaries through livelihood programs, community development, and inclusive opportunities for Persons With Disabilities.

Vision

A community where every person has access to support, livelihood, dignity, and opportunities regardless of circumstance.

Goals

To strengthen partnerships, support PWD communities, provide meaningful livelihood programs, and serve as a bridge between communities and institutions.

Meet the Founder

Founded on service, inclusion, and community empowerment.

Ms. Ruette Remegio

Founder, Kilometro Zero Organization Incorporated

Ms. Ruette Remegio is the founder of Kilometro Zero Organization Incorporated, leading initiatives focused on livelihood development, community empowerment, and inclusive opportunities for Persons With Disabilities.

Under her leadership, the organization has grown to serve more than 20,000 members and beneficiaries and has become recognized for its impact on livelihood and community development.
